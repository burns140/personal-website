Gunsmith is a bot for my destiny 2 clan's server that helps with both clan management and gameplay tips. You can send a DM to all members, and send a welcome message to any new members that join. You can also enter an item or character name to get relevant lore. For gameplay, you can enter a weapon name and you will receive recommended weapon rolls for whichever game mode you requested or you can, more broadly, get the best weapons for each weapon category.
